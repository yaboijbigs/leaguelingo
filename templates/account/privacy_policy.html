Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us for support. This includes your name, email address, fantasy football league data, and any customizations to your settings.

In the future, we may also collect your IP address automatically when you interact with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, to communicate with you, and to comply with legal obligations. Specifically, we use:

  • League data to generate fantasy football newsletters using OpenAI.
  • Email addresses to send newsletters to your league members via Sendgrid.
  • Cookies to manage user login sessions and provide a smooth login experience.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third parties in limited circumstances, such as to comply with legal requirements or to provide our services. Specifically, we share:

  • Fantasy football data with OpenAI to generate newsletters.
  • Email addresses with Sendgrid to distribute newsletters.
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe, and we do not store any payment information.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link provided in each email. If you wish to delete all your data, please contact us at support@lol.com, and we will process your request.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data indefinitely unless you explicitly request us to delete it. Once we receive a deletion request, we will remove your data from our system within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to manage user login sessions. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us identify you when you log in. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Your continued use of the service after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@lol.com.
